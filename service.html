More Value For Your Inventory

Premier Auto Group provides services to dealerships in the Used-Car industry which have a notable increase in residual value. This is brought by years of experience. Reconditioned vehicles should receive an uplift in value of 3x the amount spent in the reconditioning process. This number is derived from numerous studies worldwide and is to be used as a rule of thumb. Below, you will find a list of services which will aid in that increase of value.
